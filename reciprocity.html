CTO Club Reciprocity Program

Attend a meeting with CTOs from other cities

If you are a {{ site.title }} member and happen to be in one of cities below, you are welcomed and encouraged to join a meeting.

Before attending a meeting, you must:

  • Receive confirmation from local organizers before the meeting begins
  • Familiarize yourself with the local customs and specific cultural anthropologies (eg. dress code)
  • Acknowledge that you are not permitted to pitch your product or services
  • Acknolwedge that all discussions are understood to be governed by strict confidentiality--known as the Chatham House Rule

Send an email to {{ site.email }} to get in touch with our partner organizers.
